📘 **__Notes__** 📘
○ When codexing the T8 and T10 Pumpkinhead raids, they're both immune to all 11 common statuses (including Petrify!), **as well as Def and Res <:sd:568940821005729823> and <:dd:978982908821925938>.** You might want to do immunities in multiple runs, bringing pets that proc the <:dd:978982908821925938> debuffs in later runs.
○ It's advised to smack up the T4 raid boss! While it might seem worthless, it drops loads of great things like **Small Bestial Potions** (+5% to pet act for 1 hour!), **Ichor** (cures all debuffs, useful for redliners/blueliners), and **Great Monster Remains**!
○ Nabbing some **Hallowed Candles** from Pumpkinheads might be a good idea if you're in the mood to XP grind. While they only give a modest +5% to XP, with multiple boosts up, this adds up quickly!
○ The **Old Hallowed Blade** is one of the highest base attack weapons in the game. Building it with some Trevelyan's Charms is a good idea, but for those who've seen Pumpkinhead before, **this drop is now only available in the T10 Arisen version.** This makes T8 Pumpkinhead codexable at T8!
○ The T8 Pumpkinhead pet has an insanely high block chance, at 10%. This is the highest in the game!
○ Craft the items necesary to spawn the **Pumpkinless** raids in the Herbalist! These guys have the same status immunities as their headed versions.
